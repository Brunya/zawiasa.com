Hey, we're Robert and Bruno Zawiasa from Hungary. We believe that we live in the best age of humanity and we are the first generation of internet. The same grind we once poured into runescape now fuels our work: building tools, organizing communities, and reinvesting.

Our main focus is guild.xyz since 2021 — gamified online communities that connect over 7 million members across 1000+ communities worldwide, backed by Coinbase Global, HackVC, and TCG.

In Budapest, we run hacker nights, sponsor hackathons, and serve as the hosts of Ethereum Hungary meetups.

We also make early crypto investments, collect domains like bank.xyz and defi.vc, and maintain a collection of graded Pokémon cards.

In 2025 we're relocating to NYC after Hungary banned defi. Sometimes you have to move to keep building what you believe in.

Zawiasa means "hinge" in Polish — the hinge on which things turn.
